Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 27, 2025

At Schedulr, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our class schedule management platform.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, profile picture, and Google account ID
  • Contact Information: Email address for account management and communication

Schedule Data

  • Course Information: Course names, class schedules, time slots, and room locations
  • Semester Details: Semester dates, schedule names, and academic calendar information
  • User Preferences: Color coding for classes and customization settings

Usage Data

  • Application Analytics: How you use Schedulr features, pages visited, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, and error logs for troubleshooting

2. How We Use Your Information

Core Service Provision

  • Creating and managing your class schedules
  • Generating and exporting ICS calendar files
  • Synchronizing your schedules across devices
  • Providing schedule preview and editing capabilities

Account Management

  • Authenticating your identity and maintaining account security
  • Enabling data persistence across sessions
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries

Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve Schedulr features
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Developing new functionality based on user needs

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

Schedulr never sells, rents, or trades your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Data Sharing

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate Schedulr (cloud hosting, analytics, email services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, with advance notice to users
  • Security Protection: To prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats to our platform

Calendar Export

When you export your schedule to ICS format, you control where this data goes. We do not track or access your calendar data once exported.

4. Data Security

Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS
  • Secure Authentication: We use Google's OAuth 2.0 for secure sign-in, we never store your Google password
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access user data
  • Regular Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security vulnerabilities and threats

Data Retention

We retain your schedule data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your schedules at any time through the application. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: View all data associated with your account through your dashboard
  • Edit: Modify or update your schedules and account information at any time
  • Delete: Remove individual schedules or delete your entire account
  • Export: Download your schedule data in ICS format or JSON format upon request

Account Management

  • Sign out to end your session securely
  • Revoke Schedulr's access to your Google account through Google's security settings
  • Contact us to request complete account deletion

6. Cookies and Tracking

Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. These are necessary for Schedulr to function properly.

Analytics

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand how users interact with Schedulr. This helps us improve the service but does not track individual users personally.

7. Children's Privacy

Schedulr is designed for educational use and may be used by students of all ages. We comply with applicable privacy laws regarding children's data:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Parents and educational institutions can contact us to review, modify, or delete a child's data
  • We implement additional safeguards for educational accounts

8. International Users

Schedulr is accessible globally. If you are using our service from outside Ghana, please note:

  • Your data may be transferred to and processed in Ghana
  • We comply with applicable international privacy frameworks
  • You have the same privacy rights regardless of your location

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, we will notify users via email or in-app notification
  • We encourage you to review this policy periodically
